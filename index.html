Our Story

From a Basement Operation to a Coaching Movement

Abuv The Par was not born in a boardroom. It was built in a basement in New York, by three entrepreneurs who were tired of the gatekeeping and the noise in the eCommerce world.

CEO Stemar Greene spent seven years as a Sports Coordinator for the NYC Department of Education before leaving to build his Amazon business full-time in 2022. Alongside co-founders Tayeb Rahim and Shofi Ahad, he developed a systematic approach to Amazon FBA wholesale that generated seven figures in revenue.

The results were undeniable. Students started asking how they did it. So they started teaching — and Abuv The Par was born. Today, the company has helped hundreds of entrepreneurs build their own Amazon businesses using the same three-step framework the founders used to build their own.

The name says it all: we help our students rise above the par — above average, above the noise, above the limitations they thought were holding them back.
